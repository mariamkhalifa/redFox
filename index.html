Our Products

Holder for Foley Catheter

Our disposable holder has an adhesive and stretchable body that is water resistant and latex-free. The soft cotton material makes the holder breathable, ensuring maximum patient comfort while maintaining stretch-ability to allow movement and sustain the quality of life. Velcro tape ensures easy unfastening when needed. The holder had a writable base surface for recording the patient’s data. One size holder fits all types of Foley catheters. Our holder is manufactured at one of the best factories in China, offering great quality satisfying Canadian standards at a substantially lower cost.

Endotracheal Tube Holder

Our endotracheal tube holder is fully adjustable to ensure a comfortable and secure fit around the patient’s head. The cotton material reduces skin irritation and maximizes the patient’s comfort. Velcro tape for easy adjustment and secure fastening. The screw allows for an easily adjustable fit of the tube regardless of size. Manufactured at one of the best factories in China, offering great quality satisfying Canadian standards at a substantially lower cost.
